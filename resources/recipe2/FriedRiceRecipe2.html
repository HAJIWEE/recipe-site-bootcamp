Easy Fried Rice

A quick fried rice recipe like you get at your favorite Chinese restaurant. Leftover rice, plus a couple of eggs, baby carrots, peas, and soy sauce is all you need. Garnish with sliced green onions, if desired.

Ingredients

 Preperation Time: 5 mins

Cooking Time: 15mins
  1. ⅔ cup chopped baby carrots
  2. ½ cup frozen green peas
  3. 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  4. 1 clove garlic, minced, or to taste (Optional)
  5. 2 large eggs
  6. 1 tablespoon soy sauce, or more to taste
  7. 2 teaspoons sesame oil, or to taste

Instructions

  1. Place carrots in a small saucepan and cover with water.
  2. Bring to a low boil and cook for 3 to 5 minutes.
  3. Remove egg, and transfer to a separate plate.
  4. Stir in peas, then immediately drain in a colander.
  5. Heat a wok over high heat.
  6. Pour in vegetable oil, then stir in carrots, peas, and garlic
  7. cook for about 30 seconds
  8. Add eggs
  9. stir quickly to scramble eggs with vegetables.
  10. Stir in cooked rice.
  11. Add soy sauce and toss rice to coat.
  12. Drizzle with sesame oil and toss again.

NOTES

If you don't have any leftover cooked rice, here's how to make it: Combine 4 cups water and 2 cups white rice in a saucepan; bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer until rice is tender and water has been absorbed, 20 to 25 minutes. Remove from the heat and let cool to room temperature. For the best fried rice results, cover and refrigerate the rice, 8 hours to overnight, before using in this recipe.
